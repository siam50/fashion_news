Q: NO:1 What are the differencess between var let and conctent?

Differencess between var let and conctent:

1. Var variables can be updated and re-declared within its scope; let variables can be updated but

2. They are all hoisted to the top of their scope. But while var variables are initialized with undefined, let and const variables are not initialized.

3. var declarations are globally scoped or function scoped while let and const are block scoped.

4. While var and let can be declared without being initialized, const must be initialized during declaration.
